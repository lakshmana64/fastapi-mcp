# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Respecting differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Taking responsibility and learning from mistakes

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory comments
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing acceptable
behavior and may take corrective action for any inappropriate behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and when an individual
is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers via security/contact channels
listed in `SECURITY.md` or by opening a private communication thread.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
